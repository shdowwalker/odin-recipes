Garden Salad

Description

A refreshing and healthy garden salad loaded with fresh vegetables and topped with a zesty vinaigrette. Perfect as a side or light meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
  2. In a large bowl, combine greens, tomatoes, cucumber, and onion.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour dressing over salad and toss to combine.
  5. Serve immediately.
