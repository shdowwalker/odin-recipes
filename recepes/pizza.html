Pizza

Description

Homemade pizza with a crisp crust, tangy tomato sauce, melty cheese, and your favorite toppings. A perfect weekend treat!

Ingredients

  • 1 ball pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced olives

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly over dough.
  4. Sprinkle cheese and toppings over the sauce.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes or until crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.
  6. Let cool slightly before slicing.
